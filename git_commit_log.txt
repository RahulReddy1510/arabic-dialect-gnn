    perf: switch from GCNConv to GATConv across all 3 layers

    GCN (no attention): 82.1% macro F1
    GAT (8 heads):      84.2% macro F1 ← final

    The improvement is concentrated on Iraqi and Maghrebi where distinctive
    phoneme pairs need larger attention weights than plain aggregation provides.
    GCN over-smooths node features — by layer 3, phoneme representations in
    GCN become indistinguishable regardless of dialect.

    - 3 × GATLayer: per_head_dim=16, concat=True → hidden stays 128

    Attention weights are (E, 8) tensors used for visualization.
    Checkpoint save/load via save_checkpoint() and from_pretrained().
